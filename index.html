FAQs

Where are you willing to work?

The one thing we can agree on after the pandemic, is that work can be done anywhere remotely. I prefer to work remote based.

How is your education background a benefit for Data Science/Analytics?

Presenting findings in a clear way to non-technical and technical audiences is a must in the field. My 10 years experience teaching to students learning difficult mathematics concepts for the first time means I am well versed in this area.

Why move from Education to Data Science/Analytics?

The beauty of mathematics is that you are able to explain complicated patterns found in data. I have always loved that aspect of taking something that is a complete mess and finding the 'solution' or 'hidden pattern'. I have also always loved technology and finding a way to make something work the way I wanted through Excel or Google Sheets. Data Science and Analytics encompasses all of those aspects and allows me to continue to challenge myself and learn every day.
